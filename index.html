Sr. Benedict Carrot Patch

Dwarf Rabbit

Male

5.5 Years Old

3 Pounds

Saint Paul

My family moved and could not keep me, so I came to Animal Humane Society to find a new home.

I'm a Rebellious Spirit! I can be quite sensitive to noise and activity levels, and new surroundings and changes in routine can be stressful for me. I require daily exercise and a variety of safe toys to prevent boredom-induced mischief. Rebellious Spirits require an experienced, patient home that is willing to teach socialization skills and provide structure.

I am not a good match for homes with children under 8 years old, and while I might enjoy the companionship of other pets eventually, please give me time to adjust. Rabbits like me are excellent candidates for agility class, but wouldn't do well in the the role of therapy animal.
